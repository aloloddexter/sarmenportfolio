Educational Background


I embarked on my educational journey at Lianga Central Elementary School, where I laid the foundation for my academic path. Although I wasn't recognized as a student with honors during my elementary years, I was determined to push myself further.


My dedication and hard work bore fruit when I entered Lianga National Comprehensive High School. From grade 7 through grade 10, I consistently earned with honors and received three prestigious awards in grade 7: Best in Science, Best in Math, and Best in Aral Pan. These recognitions fueled my desire to excel academically.


In my pursuit of knowledge, I continued my high school education at Barobo National High School. Throughout my time there, from grade 11 to grade 12, I maintained my position as an with honor student and even achieved with high honors twice during the third and fourth grading period in grade 11. These experiences deepened my commitment to academic excellence.


Currently, I'm in my third year of college at North Eastern Mindanao State University, where I'm majoring in Computer Science (BSCS). My college journey has been both challenging and rewarding as I dive into the fascinating world of technology.

Education has always been a cornerstone of my life, driving me to strive for excellence in everything I do. I'm passionate about learning and growing, and I believe that knowledge is a powerful tool that can shape our future.

I'm excited to connect with fellow learners, educators, and enthusiasts who share my passion for education and personal growth. Let's exchange ideas, discuss academic pursuits, and inspire each other to reach new heights on this educational journey.
