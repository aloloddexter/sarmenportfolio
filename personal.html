About Me

I'm Jean Alambag Sarmen, a 20-year-old from the charming town of Lianga in Surigao del Sur. Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science (BSCS) at North Eastern Mindanao State University - Lianga Campus, I'm passionate about the ever-evolving world of technology and its limitless possibilities.

When I'm not immersed in my studies, you'll often find me indulging in my favorite hobbies. I'm an avid volleyball enthusiast, always eager to catch thrilling matches and learn from the incredible athletes who inspire me. Reading books is another one of my passions, as I believe that books have the power to transport us to different worlds and expand our horizons. On lazy afternoons, I can be found engrossed in the pages of a captivating story.

But it's not just sports and literature that capture my heart; I'm also a devoted fan of Korean dramas. There's something magical about the storytelling and emotions in K-dramas that resonates with me. And of course, music is a constant companion in my life. I have an eclectic taste and find solace in the melodies that speak to my soul.
